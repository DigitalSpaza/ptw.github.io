2. Programme Overview
2.1 The Price to Win Programme ("PTW") is a national incentive programme designed to encourage responsible pricing, product availability, and execution standards.
2.2 The Programme is structured in two phases:
Phase One: Customers are assessed on affordability metrics and may earn points and stand a chance to win monthly spot prizes.
Phase Two: Customers may, subject to audit and verification, stand a chance to be entered into a random draw for the Extravaganza Event.
2.3 Participation does not guarantee any reward. All prizes are awarded via random draw.
3. Eligibility
3.1 Participation is limited to qualifying customers as determined by Heineken Beverages.
3.2 Customers must:
Hold a valid liquor licence
Be compliant with Heineken agreements
Maintain assets in good condition
Have no outstanding compliance issues
